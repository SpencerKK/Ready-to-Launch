Choosing Your Next Step

The biggest thing holding podcasts back from becoming successful podcasts is the fact that most of them never get made. Over the years, countless people have approached me with truly great ideas for podcasts they want to start — but none of those ideas are ever made into shows. The reasons for this included a fear of public failure, the lack of spare time, or even believing they had “nothing unique or valuable to offer.”


It’s time to step out of your own way! Your community is waiting for you.


So often, we are our own greatest obstacles. I get it — but the most important step to take is the one right in front of you.


We will work together and offer you the proper tools, guidance, support, and network that will ensure a successful launch of your show that you can be proud of

Launch

  • Solidify your show’s concept, format, and the types of guests you are interested in speaking to
  • Produce and launch your first episode, obtaining approval for your show to all podcasting platforms
  • Configure your show’s RSS feed for effective publishing
  • Have in-depth discussions regarding the direction of your brand, keeping you accountable, on track and aligned with the vision for your show and the community that follows
  • Implement streamlined posting strategies and establish a targeted posting schedule

Grow

  • Connect with high-quality guests that align with their show’s values
  • Connect with other podcasters
  • Receive show audits and subsequent consultations wherein we clarify and refine both the direction of the show and the brand that surrounds it
  • Implement growth strategies and increase the reach of your show
  • Establish a community around their show and website
  • Identify potential sponsors that reflect the values of their show
  • Develop effective strategies for pitching to ideal sponsors
