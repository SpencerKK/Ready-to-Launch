The Up-and-Coming Female Voices That Are Reshaping The Podcasting Industry

Still, as podcasting is new, there remains a gap in the industry where there could be community, support and collaboration. This is limiting for those that decide to pursue podcasting, but ultimately give up because of the difficulties they face in the process of launching and growing their shows.

The Ready to Launch Podcasts network is the first podcasting network for up-and-coming female podcasters who have shown promise in their mission and ability to communicate their message. We address the gap by connecting and working with podcasters ready to:

  • Expand their reach through cross-collaboration with other shows in the RTL network
  • Share their message to the built-in audiences of other shows in the RTL network through cross-sponsorships and guest appearances
  • Conceptualize and plan interviews around vetted individuals, brands and companies that have pitched themselves to the RTL network and are deemed a good fit for their show
  • Participate in digital seminars with trained media/podcasting experts and other members of the RTL network
  • Work toward gaining the best possible sponsorship opportunities with the support of Ready to Launch
