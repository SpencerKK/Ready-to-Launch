RTL & You

The Ready to Launch network is the first podcast network for emerging female podcasters who are dedicated to the growth and development of their shows. Our network supports the growth of its creators by connecting them to other podcasters, guests, and sponsorship opportunities. The purpose of the Ready to Launch network is to help transition passionate podcasters into profitable (and passionate) podcasters. We place the greatest emphasis on cultivating a larger sense of community and collaboration within the podcasting industry.
Get in touch to see if your podcasting goals align with our network. I look forward to speaking about your show!

  • Expand their reach through cross-collaboration with other shows in the RTL network
  • Share their message to the built-in audiences of other shows in the RTL network through cross-sponsorships and guest appearances
  • Conceptualize and plan interviews around vetted individuals, brands and companies that have pitched themselves to the RTL network and are deemed a good fit for their show
  • Participate in digital seminars with trained media/podcasting experts and other members of the RTL network
  • Work toward gaining the best possible sponsorship opportunities with the support of Ready to Launch Podcasts
